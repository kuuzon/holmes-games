About Holmes Games

Discover the minds behind the mysteries

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of puzzle enthusiasts and game designers, Holmes Games was born from a simple idea: what if every puzzle told a story? What if solving a riddle felt like uncovering a genuine mystery?

Named in honor of the world's most famous fictional detective, our studio is dedicated to creating puzzle games that challenge the intellect while captivating the imagination. We believe that the best puzzles are those that make you feel like a detective, piecing together clues until the final revelation.

From our humble beginnings in a small London office to our current headquarters serving millions of players worldwide, we've never lost sight of our original mission: to craft mysteries that matter.

Our Philosophy

At Holmes Games, we follow three core principles:

1. Every Clue Matters - We never include red herrings for the sake of frustration. Every piece of information in our games serves a purpose, rewarding attentive players.

2. Accessible Challenge - Our games welcome newcomers while providing depth for veterans. We believe the joy of solving a puzzle should be available to everyone.

3. Story-Driven Design - Puzzles exist within rich narratives. You're never just solving a puzzle; you're unraveling a mystery.
